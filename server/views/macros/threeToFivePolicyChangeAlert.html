Due to a policy change, prisoners are now considered for open conditions when they’re within 5 years of their conditional release date (CRD).

This means that when a prisoner’s CRD:

  • is in the next 5 years, they must be reviewed every 6 months
  • is more than 5 years away, they must be reviewed every 12 months

Consider this when setting their next review date.
